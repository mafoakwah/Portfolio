Data Exploration

(Deluxe's Sales_Export_2019_2020)

The dataset that will be used for this project is based on the online retailer Deluxes's sales. Deluxe is an online retailer based in the UK. Here we will explore the dataset and the attributes associated with Deluxe such as the country, category, sales, costs, Overall Profit. The goal in this project is to create visualizations in order to gain insight of Deluxes Sales in 2019 and 2020.
