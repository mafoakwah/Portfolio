Abstract

This project is based on an Investors desire to invest in Video Games. Before the investor can invest, it is our job to investigate where the investor should best invest their money in a means that will ultimately benefit the investor. To accomplish this, we use a Video Game data set from 2010 to 2016. We will prepare the data set by exploring and cleaning the data set to create proper visualizations and models for the investor. At the end of this project, we will explain to the investor our findings based on our visualizations and models.

1. Scenario

Suppose there is an investor who wants to invest in a gaming platform. However, before the investor funds, they want to know which gaming platform between the years 2010 to 2016 has the best NA, EU, and Global sales. The investor also wants to know what video game category produces the most income between 2010 and 2016 for each video game platform so that they can better invest their money to better profit themselves. The investor also wants to see what game developers create more profitable games and the video game categories those developers mainly develop. The investor wants an in-depth analysis of video game platforms, categories, and developers before investing money.
